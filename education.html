Southern State Community College GPA (3.88)

During my time at Southern State Community College, I was actively involved in leadership, technology support, and student engagement initiatives. I served as the Phi Theta Kappa chapter president, IT laboratory assistant, student eSports manager, and HALO eSports captain. Through these roles, I gained experience in student leadership, team management, and technical support while contributing to campus life and student development.

As Phi Theta Kappa chapter president, I led initiatives that earned international and regional recognition, organized multiple induction ceremonies, and developed reports that influenced college administration decisions. I also coordinated volunteer efforts and student-led projects to foster leadership opportunities. As an IT laboratory assistant, I managed Active Directory, assisted students with projects, created 3D models, and helped develop instructional resources to support learning. In my role as student eSports manager, I oversaw varsity teams, facilitated recruitment, improved team communications, and played a key role in transitioning to a new competitive league. Additionally, as HALO eSports captain, I collaborated with teammates, led training sessions, and helped expand the program by recruiting new players and supporting logistics for competitions. My experiences at Southern State allowed me to build leadership, organizational, and technical skills while positively impacting the student community.
